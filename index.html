Services

Achilles Tendon Repair
Price per appointement: $75

Achilles Tendon Repair
Goals for Achilles Reconstruction:
  • Allow the tendon to heal, reduce pain, swelling and inflammation
  • Restore tendon and muscles to original strength and flexibility
  • Gradually return to normal activity and training levels.
  • As rehab progresses goals are based on your bodies response.

ACL Reconstruction
Price per appointement: $90

ACL Reconstruction
Goals for ACL Reconstruction:
  • Fix the anterior cruciate ligament.
  • Decrease swelling and obtain full control/extension of the knee.
  • Strengthening using closed chain kinetic exercises and balance exercises.
  • Progressive work on core muscles.

Arthritis Therapy
Price per appointement: $55

Arthritis Therapy
Goals for Arthritis Therapy:
  • Improve pain.
  • Improve range of motion and mobility.
  • Reduce swelling.
  • Prevent fractures and further injuries.

TMJ Pain Therapy
Price per appointement: $60

TMJ Pain Therapy
Goals for TMJ Pain Therapy:
  • Reduce pain and tenderness in jaw area.
  • Prevent jaw from locking into fixed position.
  • Treatement with oral splint or mouth guard.
  • Perform stretches to strengthen jaw muscles.

Vestibular Rehabilitation
Price per appointement: $40

Vestibular Rehabilitation
Goals for Vestibular Rehabilitation:
  • Perform vision stability training.
  • Stretching and strengthening exercises.
  • Improved ability to stabilize vision/gaze.
  • Increase in confidence in ability to maintain balance.
